About



Based in San Antonio, Jackie Not Marilyn blends modern pop, funk, rock, and throwbacks into a seamless, high‑energy show. Our lineup scales from an acoustic duo to a full band with optional horns, so we fit patios, ballrooms, and festival stages alike. We’ve held residencies at local staples and bring our own pro sound, lighting, and MC services to keep timelines tight and dance floors full. Whether it’s an elegant wedding, a high‑stakes corporate event, or a community festival, we handle the details — custom requests, announcements, and smooth transitions — so you can relax and celebrate. From the first email to the last encore, we make it easy, fun, and unforgettable.

Additional Value
Production

We invest in tour-grade sound and lighting so your event looks and sounds incredible—clear vocals, balanced mix, and tasteful lighting that photographs beautifully. We handle MC/announcements, carry backup gear, and coordinate with your venue so everything runs smooth from first note to last.

Preparation

Every show is custom-curated: we build the set around your guests, timeline, and tastes—even learning special requests ahead of time. We’ll confirm details in advance (pronunciations, cues, must-plays/no-plays) so the night feels personal and seamless.

Flexibility

Patio or ballroom, cocktail hour or full-tilt dance floor—our lineup scales from acoustic duo to full band (with optional horns). We match volume, dress code, and pacing to your event, and we’re happy to collaborate on unique moments to make it unmistakably yours.
