About



We’re live music experts, wedding enthusiasts, and party starters. We’re a premium, customizable band specializing in high-energy music for festivals, weddings, corporate events, and private functions–at a sensible price. We infuse every event with energy and fun and pride ourselves on filling dance floors.

The Hitz provide live band services for any event. From wedding entertainment, to corporate parties, to a more intimate band or classic ceremony music, we offer an all-inclusive entertainment service.
